Shipping Protocols

Dispatch Timing: All artifacts are dispatched within 2-4 business days.

Global Transit: We ship worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs duties which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Tracking: Once your ritual object has left our facility, you will receive a tracking code to monitor its journey through the concrete jungle.

Returns & Exchanges

The 30-Day Window: If the artifact does not align with your vibe, you have 30 days from receipt to initiate a return.

Condition: Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging. We cannot accept items that have been altered or damaged.

Process: To start a return, contact us at support@ramenzoo.com with your order number. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective.
